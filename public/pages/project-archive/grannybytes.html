Granny Bytes

Granny Bytes is a project that explores inter-generational connections and how it can manifest within our interactions with technology.

Collaborator: Robin Altman, Christine Chang, Hannah Lee

Javascript | Node.js | Google AI and openAI | 2024

Github Devpost

Within this project we were researching the capabilities of AI and its potential relationship with both the elderly population and younger generation's interactions with them. Granny Bytes is a proposed method for bridging the gap between the two, and also creating a sort of permanence for the fleeting nature defining this relationship.

Hi, I'm Paridhi – a creative technologist currently based in New York and studying at Parsons School of Design. My work delves into how memories influence our digital interactions. What is a person's digital footprint? How do they feel when interacting with technology? These are some questions that play in my mind. I use different mediums to explore my observed environment and lived experiences to create immersive and interactive experiences. Viewing the web as a digital archive of a person’s life, exploring human to machine interactions through various lenses and tinkering with emerging technologies always keeps me going.

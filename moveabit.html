Exhibited at the Microscope Gallery for the Quantum Art exhibition organised by IBM and The New School

Move a Bit is a live motion capture project bringing quantum to life through an interactive display that visually showcases entanglement. Just as a special correlation between qubits exists, the particles in Move a Bit have a special relationship with the subject in motion. Movement produces the quantum state with all quantum possibilities represented in different colors. Stillness represents the observed collapsed, classical state.
